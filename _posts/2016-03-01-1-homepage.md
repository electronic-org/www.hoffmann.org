# Austria Center Vienna

* Austria’s largest conference centre 
* 7 minutes from the city centre (Metro)
* capacity for 20,000 delegates 
* 24 lecture rooms (100-4,320 pax)
* 180 meeting rooms
* 22,000 m² of exhibition space.
